Welcome to a brief introduction of the video game:

Katamari Damacy is a quirky, third-person action-puzzle game where you roll a magical, sticky ball called a katamari to collect everything in your path (anything from thumbtacks to people!) growing bigger to recreate the stars the King of All Cosmos accidentally destroyed.

Much of the game's charm comes from the recognizable low-poly style, the fun variety of assets, and a wonderfully composed soundtrack. The soundtrack was released in Japan as Katamari Fortissimo Damacy.

Originally created, developed, and released in Japan on March 18, 2004 by Namco for the PlayStation 2.
